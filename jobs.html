SSC GD Examination Overview
Exam Name SSC Constable (General Duty) Exam
Conducting Body Staff Selection Commission (SSC)
Exam Level National Level
Job Location All India
Educational Qualification 10th Pass
Category Government Jobs
Age Limit 18-23 years
Exam Mode Online
Selection Stages Computer-Based Exam (CBE), Physical Efficiency Test (PET), Physical Standard Test (PST), Medical Exam (Detailed Medical Exam)
Exam Frequency Not fixed (based on the number of vacancies intimated by the Ministry of Home Affairs to SSC)
Application Fee INR 100
Payscale Pay Level-3 (INR 21,700-69,100)
Exam Purpose Selection of candidates for Constables (General Duty) in different forces like CRPF, BSF, CISF, SSB, ITBP, AR, SSF, and NIA
Exam Language English and Hindi
Exam Helpdesk No. 011-24363343
Official Website http://ssc.gov.in/
Selection Process Written Examination: Computer-Based Test (CBT), 90 minutes duration, Objective-type questions.
Physical Efficiency Test (PET): Male candidates - 5 km race in 24 minutes; Female candidates - 1.6 km race in 8.30 minutes.
Physical Standard Test (PST): Measurement of height, chest, and weight.
Medical Examination: Must meet medical standards.
Document Verification: Verification of educational and other relevant documents.
Exam Pattern General Intelligence & Reasoning: 25 questions
General Knowledge & General Awareness: 25 questions
Elementary Mathematics: 25 questions
English/Hindi: 25 questions
Total Marks: 100
Negative Marking: 0.25 marks for each wrong answer
Syllabus General Intelligence & Reasoning: Analogies, Classification, Series, Coding-Decoding, Blood Relations, Directions, Similarities and Differences, Statement and Conclusions.
General Knowledge & General Awareness: Current Affairs, Indian History, Indian Geography, Indian Economy, General Science, Culture and Sports.
Elementary Mathematics: Number Systems, Simplification, Average, Percentage, Ratio and Proportion, Interest, Profit and Loss, Time and Distance, Time and Work.
English/Hindi: Grammar, Vocabulary, Sentence Structure, Comprehension, Synonyms and Antonyms, Idioms and Phrases.
Application Process Notification: Check SSC’s official website for updates.
Application Form: Available online on the SSC website.
Application Fee: Generally a nominal fee; exemption for female candidates and reserved categories as per government rules.
Important Dates Notification Release: Usually in the last quarter of the year.
Application Start Date: A few weeks after the notification.
Application Deadline: Typically within a month from the start date.
Exam Date: Announced in the notification or on SSC’s website.
Preparation Tips Understand the Exam Pattern, Study Regularly, Practice Previous Papers, Stay Updated with Current Affairs, and Focus on Physical Fitness.
Career Prospects Roles include maintaining law and order, security, and performing duties related to border management and internal security. Opportunities for career growth and promotions based on performance and service years.
